About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in Computer Science Department at Rutgers University under the supervision of Professor Dimitris Metaxas. Before that, I received an M.S degree from Graduate Univ. of Chinese Academy of Sciences and obtained a B.E degree from University of Electronic Science and Technology of China. I worked at Cleerly, LightInTheBox, and Baidu. My research interests include machine learning and computer vision, involving the topics:
  • Perception: Image Segmentation and Classification
  • Generative Adversarial Network
